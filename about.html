Our Story

Building South Africa Since 2004

Abaphumeleli Trading 57 CC was founded in 2004 with a clear mission — to deliver high-quality construction services that uplift communities and meet the infrastructure needs of South Africa's growing municipalities.

The name "Abaphumeleli" means "The Successful Ones" in isiZulu — a reflection of our commitment to successful project delivery on every engagement, regardless of scale or complexity.

Over the past two decades, we have grown from a small regional contractor into a trusted partner for local government, insurance companies, and property managers across KwaZulu-Natal, Gauteng, Mpumalanga, and the Eastern Cape.

Our portfolio includes new municipal building construction, water treatment infrastructure, property maintenance, and rapid insurance reinstatement — all delivered with the same standard of professionalism, transparency, and speed.

What We Do

Three Core Disciplines, One Reliable Partner

We specialise in three interconnected areas of construction and property services, allowing us to serve as a single point of contact for clients across the public and private sector.

Civil Construction

Municipal buildings, earthworks, stormwater drainage, access roads, and sports facilities for government and private clients.

Insurance Reinstatement

24/7 emergency response for fire, flood, and storm damage with end-to-end claims management and insurer liaison.

Property Maintenance

Painting, plumbing, roofing, waterproofing, and preventative maintenance for schools, offices, and residential complexes.
